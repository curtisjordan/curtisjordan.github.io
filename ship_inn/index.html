About

Ship Inn

In the Picturesque village of Sewerby overlooking the bay between the seaside town of Bridlington and fishing village of Flamborough. The Ship Inn Sewerby is a traditional pub tucked well away off the main road, but can be spotted far more easily from the cliff top. Located next to Sewerby Hall and Gardens.

Serving fine food and award winning ales, beers and wines with a children's play area and large car park. The pub is Family orientated and welcomes dogs on a lead into the pub. Inside there's lots of dark wood, log fires, intimate cubby holes and a friendly atmosphere.

Owners

Passionate and dedicated to his love of food. Charlie Kilburn took the reigns of The Ship Inn back in October 2006, since then he has poured his heart and soul into making The Ship Inn a beautiful country lane pub and restaurant.

Along with Lou and his family he has developed a strong relationship with customers far and wide, making them feel welcomed and comfortable throughout their stay in Sewerby and the surrounding areas.
